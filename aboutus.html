We are los pollos hermanos

Founded in 1997 we have been satisfying the stomachs of the city of Albuquerque for 25 years

Our Founder Gus Fring was born in 1960 and came to America hoping to become successful. Through totally legit means he gathered funds for his first los pollos hermanos location. And after alot of hard work and dedication he has become very sucessful and opened 14 total locations.

Our mission is to be a positive impact on the Albuquerque community. Providing good food, good paying jobs and donations and aid to those who need it.
